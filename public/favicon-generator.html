How to Create Favicon:

  1. Right-click on your preferred favicon above
  2. Select "Inspect Element" to capture the rendered icon
  3. Use browser dev tools to take a screenshot of just the favicon
  4. Or use an online favicon generator like favicon.io with these designs
  5. Save as favicon.ico in multiple sizes (16x16, 32x32, 48x48)
  6. Replace the current favicon in your app folder

Alternative Method:

Visit favicon.io and use:

  • Text: "GD"
  • Background: Linear gradient from #00b894 to #e17055
  • Font: Bold, white color
  • Shape: Circle
